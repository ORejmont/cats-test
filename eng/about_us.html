ABOUT US

We are based near Nymburk and we decided to start our station after one bad experience. Some years ago, I bought a British cat. Unfortunately, I was a novice and I found after the purchase, that I bought a cat, that was from an unprofessional breeding room. Later, the cat started to show signs of suffering from various diseases, such as problems with the urinary tract, kidneys, bad teeth and other. Despite all the veterinary care, we were not able to save the cat. And the cat went to the cat's heaven. It was a huge emotional loss for me, not to mention the financial cost of veterinary care.


In a short time, we bought another cat, but this time, the cat came from a breeding station. We named it Dasha, after which we named our breeding station Dasha, CZ. In our breeding station, our cats are tested for FIV – feline AIDS, FeLV – feline leukemia, PKD – kidney disease, HCM – heart disease. Since these diseases are genetical, we make sure, that none of our kittens might inherit any of these diseases. We carefully choose parents of the kittens so that the lineage is "clean" and there was no crossbreeding within the family.


All cats enjoy an above-standard care in the form of quality food, enough space to live and loving care. We feed cats with brands: N&D, Royal Canin, Cosma, Hill’s and Applaws. Our motto is that our kittens leave our station after 3 months healthy, pampered and fully socialized. The kittens go to new families after the second vaccination, they are dewormed, chipped and with a pedigree. It makes a huge difference if one of the "breeders" does not act according to the laws of breeding and does not pay attention to the above-mentioned examinations, the clean line or the number of litters. An adult cat can have a maximum of 3 litters in 2 years.


Our motto: If you do something with passion and for a long time, you do it well.
